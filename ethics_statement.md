
  ### Edward's AI Ethics
  

        These are my own words, typed with my own fingers...

  
  ### These are a set of principles that I will follow as I advance in the field of AI engineering.


      1. AI should elevate and augment the human condition.

      2. AI should not be used to completely replace human interaction.

      3. How AI is built and how it works should be deeply understood by all who use it.

      4. AI should not be used by those in power to devalue humanity and the importance of coexistence.

      5. AI development processes should be designed in a way as to prioritize human-in-the-loop interaction and deliver outcomes that accentuate human values (varied as they may be).

      6. AI should, through the process of understanding it, increase the critical thinking and the self-reflecting skills of the human being. Because after all, we programmed it to learn based on what we learned about how we learn.
